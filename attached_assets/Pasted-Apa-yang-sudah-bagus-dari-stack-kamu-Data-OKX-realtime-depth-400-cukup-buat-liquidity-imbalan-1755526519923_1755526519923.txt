Apa yang sudah bagus dari stack kamu
	•	Data OKX realtime + depth 400 → cukup buat liquidity/imbalance/whale wall.
	•	Candles multi-TF → bahan BOS/CHoCH/OB/FVG & konfirmasi MTF.
	•	Modul SMC (zones/analysis) → bisa mapping level presisi.
	•	Signals (tajam & quick) → 2 gaya sinyal (naratif & rule-based).
	•	News & sentiment → layer fundamental cepat.
	•	Performance/backtest/report → landasan evaluasi objektif.
	•	Promptbook/context → konsistensi output & formatting ke Telegram.

Kenapa tetap tak akan “sempurna”
	•	Stokastik & regime shift: perilaku market berubah (volatilitas, likuiditas).
	•	Microstructure noise: spoofing, latency, slippage, spread.
	•	Data bias: backtest look-ahead/overfit kalau guardrail nggak ketat.
	•	Eksekusi: sinyal bagus bisa gagal kalau fill jelek/routing lambat.

Biar “kelas institusi”: checklist peningkatan (prioritas)
	1.	Rule SMC yang bisa diaudit
	•	Definisikan BOS/CHoCH/OB/FVG secara deterministik (no ambiguity).
	•	Simpan state per simbol/TF (struktur terakhir, swing HH/HL/LL/LH).
	2.	Confluence Engine (skor 0–100)
Gabungkan: SMC (40), orderbook imbalance (20), volatilitas & ATR (10), momentum (EMA/RSI/MACD 15), funding/OI (10), news spike (5). Threshold sinyal tajam mis. ≥70.
	3.	Risk & position sizing
	•	SL berbasis struktur (di bawah OB/atas supply) + ATR buffer.
	•	Sizing dinamis (vol-scaled), max risk 0.5–1.0% per trade.
	•	Hard stop + time-stop (mis. 45–90 menit untuk intraday).
	4.	Execution-aware
	•	Hitung expected slippage dari depth saat entry; tolak sinyal kalau slippage > X bps.
	•	Cek spread & book stability (hindari saat book kosong/spread melebar).
	5.	Regime & filter
	•	Volatility regime (ATR/σ rolling): hanya ambil sinyal saat kondisi cocok.
	•	Funding/OI: hindari entry berlawanan saat funding ekstrim + OI meledak.
	6.	Walk-forward + out-of-sample
	•	Backtest rolling (6m train → 1m test), no look-ahead.
	•	Simpan metrik per periode untuk deteksi decay.
	7.	Live metrics & guardrail
	•	Dashboard: win-rate 30D, PF, Sharpe, avg RR, max DD, avg slippage, avg time-to-profit.
	•	Circuit breaker: pause sinyal jika 3–4 loss beruntun atau DD harian > X%.
	8.	Alert hygiene (Telegram)
	•	Sinyal = single message + update thread (move to BE, partial TP, SL-to-BE).
	•	Sertakan: status (Long/Short), entry/SL/TP, alasan singkat (BOS + OB + imbalance), ID sinyal.
	9.	Data sanity
	•	Deteksi gap/NaN/lag; kalau API lambat → fallback cache; label sinyal “stale” jika data > N detik.
	10.	Self-improvement loop

	•	Log: fitur saat entry + outcome → retrain skor confluence (simple logistic/XGBoost).
	•	Tuning threshold otomatis per simbol/TF.

Cara menilai “bagus” secara objektif (acceptance criteria)
	•	Win-rate ≥ 48% dengan RR rata2 ≥ 1.6 (PF ≥ 1.3) dalam 60 hari.
	•	Max DD 30D ≤ 8%, Sharpe 30D ≥ 1.0.
	•	Avg slippage ≤ 3 bps di pair utama.
	•	Latency sinyal ≤ 500 ms dari tick ke publikasi.
	•	85% sinyal “tajam” mencapai at least TP1; 50–60% ke TP2 pada regime normal.
